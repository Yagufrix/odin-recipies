Chilaquiles

Chilaquiles rojos is one of many typical dishes that Mexico have

It is made of fried tortillas with some hot and spicy sauce

This are the following ingredients:

  • 12 corn tortillas
  • Conr oil
  • Salt
  • 1 /12 to 2 cup red chile sauce or salsa verde
  • A few springs of epazote

Steps to follow:

  1. Fry the tortillas
  2. Heat the salsa
  3. Add fried tortillas to the salsa
  4. Optional: You can add a fried egg if you want it!
